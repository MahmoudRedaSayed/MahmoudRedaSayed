## About Me

I'm a **Senior Backend Developer** based in **Cairo, Egypt**, with a **Bachelor's in Computer Engineering** from Cairo University (GPA 3.2/4.0). I specialize in building **scalable backend systems**, **RESTful APIs**, and **fintech platforms** that demand high performance, reliability, and security.

Currently at [**PaySky**](https://www.linkedin.com/company/paysky), I work on microservices architecture and backend services for fintech products. I also contribute as a **Teaching Assistant** at Cairo University.

I enjoy designing clean architectures, optimizing databases, and collaborating across frontend and mobile teams — with hands-on experience in React and Next.js on the full-stack side.
